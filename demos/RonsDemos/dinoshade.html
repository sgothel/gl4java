Dinoshade applet
Originially written by Mark J. Kilgard
Ported to Java by Ron Cemer
Press and hold left mouse button, then drag to rotate view.
Press and hold left mouse button, then press and hold right mouse button, then drag to rotate light.
Click right mouse button for a menu of options.
NOTE: If your system does not support hardware-accelerated stenciling, this demo will run slowly unless you disable stenciling for shadows and reflections.
